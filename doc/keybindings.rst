===========
Keybindings
===========

Placing Components
==================
+------------+------------------------+
| Keybinding |         Useage         |
+============+========================+
| ``c``      | Place a Capacitor      |
+------------+------------------------+
| ``l``      | Place an Inductor      |
+------------+------------------------+
| ``r``      | Place a Resistor       |
+------------+------------------------+
| ``w``      | Place a Wire           |
+------------+------------------------+
| ``i``      | Place a Current Source |
+------------+------------------------+
| ``v``      | Place a Voltage Source |
+------------+------------------------+
| ``p``      | Place an Open Port     |
+------------+------------------------+
